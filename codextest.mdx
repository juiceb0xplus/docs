        <h1>1. Foundation & Philosophy</h1>
        
        <h2>1.1 Core Philosophy: Cognitive Symbiosis</h2>
        <div class="axiom-box">
            <p>The Project Codex operates as a <strong>living cognitive substrate</strong> where human creativity and AI systematization merge into a unified project consciousness. It represents neither pure human thought nor pure machine logic, but rather a synthesized intelligence that captures the essence of both.</p>
        </div>
        
        <h2>1.2 Fundamental Axioms</h2>
        <div class="info-box">
            <ol>
                <li><strong>Knowledge is Stateful:</strong> Every piece of information exists in a lifecycle state (embryonic → proposed → accepted → implemented → deprecated)</li>
                <li><strong>Context is Paramount:</strong> No information exists in isolation; every atom of knowledge maintains explicit relationships to its conceptual neighbors</li>
                <li><strong>Synthesis Supersedes Storage:</strong> The system's value lies not in what it stores, but in how it transforms raw thought into structured wisdom</li>
                <li><strong>The Manifest is Sacred:</strong> A single, always-current entry point provides complete project orientation in under 1000 words</li>
            </ol>
        </div>
        
        <h2>1.3 The Three Pillars</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Temporal Coherence:</strong> Past decisions inform present state which constrains future possibilities</li>
            <li><strong>Semantic Density:</strong> Maximum meaning in minimum volume through aggressive synthesis and compression</li>
            <li><strong>Bidirectional Accessibility:</strong> Equally optimized for human intuition and machine parsing</li>
        </ul>
